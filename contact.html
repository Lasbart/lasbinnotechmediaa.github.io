Frequently Asked Questions

How long does printing take?

Most standard printing jobs are completed within 24 hours. Large or complex orders may take 2-3 days. Rush orders can be accommodated for an additional fee.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! We offer competitive pricing for bulk orders, especially for t-shirts, mugs, and other custom items. Contact us for a custom quote.

Can I bring my own design?

Absolutely! You can provide your own designs in formats like JPG, PNG, or PDF. We can also help with basic design adjustments if needed.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, mobile money (all networks), and bank transfers. Payment is typically required before or upon completion of your order.

Do you deliver?

Yes, we offer delivery services within Awutu Bereku and surrounding areas for an additional fee based on location.

What if I'm not satisfied with the result?

Customer satisfaction is our priority. If there's an issue with our work, we'll work with you to make it right. Contact us immediately if you have concerns.
